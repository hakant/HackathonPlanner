crazybeeliuzhe

Conducting interview on IoT

Interviewers sometimes are not trustable and sending them around is a costly and time consuming process.The answers to lots of simple questions like which type of coffee is mostly selected?, which brand of candy is the most popular purchased?, which door is opened more often in the office?, what is the best temperature in the building? etc are already reflected by human behavior and don't need be collected through the tedious process. To answer those questions we don't need to send interviewers around to waste time an money. With the help of sensors and IoT, we can collect such kind of information more efficiently and quickly.Focus: DIY. Inspiration: Prototype a interviewing system (or data collector) on embed devices to collect Anonymous data automatically. Using a raspberry pi to prototype the idea. I have the pi, If someone can donate sensors to connect to GPIO that will be even nicer.
